# NiceGo Privacy Policy / NiceGo 隐私政策

_Last updated / 最近更新: 2026-05-31_

---

## English

NiceGo ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect through the NiceGo mobile application (the "App"), how we use it, and the choices you have.

### 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use the App:

**a. Account information**
- Phone number and password (when you register with phone)
- Apple ID identifier and email (when you sign in with Apple)
- Nickname, avatar, and profile information you provide

**b. Content you create**
- Posts, comments, replies, photos, videos, and other content you upload
- Direct messages and group chat messages
- Activities and events you create or join
- Q&A questions and answers, course content, product reviews

**c. Transaction information**
- Order details, addresses, and payment status when you make purchases through the App

**d. Device and usage information**
- Device model, OS version, language, and time zone
- Crash reports and basic interaction logs used to diagnose issues
- IP address (recorded automatically by our servers when requests are made)

**e. Location information**
- Approximate or precise location, only when you grant permission and only for features that require it (e.g. discovering nearby activities)

**f. Camera and photo library**
- We access your camera and photo library only when you choose to take a photo or pick one to upload. We do not scan your library in the background.

**g. Push notifications**
- A device push token may be stored so we can deliver activity, chat, and order notifications. You may disable push at any time in iOS Settings.

### 2. How We Use Your Information

- To create and maintain your account and authenticate sessions
- To deliver the social, chat, activity, and commerce features you request
- To process orders and provide customer support
- To detect, prevent and respond to fraud, abuse and security issues
- To send service-related notifications

### 3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:
- With other users, to the extent you publish content or send messages
- With service providers strictly needed to run the App (hosting, push delivery, payment processing)
- When required by applicable law or to protect rights and safety

### 4. Data Retention

We retain your account data while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time at the contact below; we will delete or anonymize within a reasonable period unless retention is required by law.

### 5. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, hashed passwords, access controls) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

### 6. Children

NiceGo is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have done so, we will delete it.

### 7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, or to object to or restrict certain processing. Contact us using the email below to make a request.

### 8. International Users

NiceGo is operated from China. By using the App you understand that your data may be processed in China. We apply the protections described in this policy regardless of where you are located.

### 9. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted in the App or sent via email.

### 10. Contact

For questions about this Policy or to exercise your rights, contact:
**nicegoprivacy@gmail.com**

## 中文

NiceGo（"我们"）尊重您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们通过 NiceGo 移动应用（"本应用"）收集哪些个人数据、如何使用，以及您拥有的选择权。

### 一、我们收集的信息

**a. 账号信息**
- 手机号注册时的手机号与密码
- 通过 Apple 登录时的 Apple ID 标识与邮箱
- 您提供的昵称、头像与资料

**b. 您创建的内容**
- 帖子、评论、回复、图片、视频等上传内容
- 私聊与群聊消息
- 您创建或参加的活动
- 问答、课程内容、商品评价

**c. 交易信息**
- 订单详情、收货地址、支付状态等下单信息

**d. 设备与使用信息**
- 设备型号、操作系统版本、语言与时区
- 崩溃日志与基本交互日志，用于排障
- 服务器在接收请求时自动记录的 IP 地址

**e. 位置信息**
- 仅在您授权且某些功能需要时（如发现附近的活动）使用大致或精确位置

**f. 相机与相册**
- 仅在您主动拍照或选择上传图片时访问相机与相册，不会在后台扫描您的相册

**g. 推送通知**
- 我们可能存储设备推送令牌以推送活动、聊天与订单通知。您可在 iOS 系统设置中随时关闭

### 二、信息的使用

- 创建和维护账号、登录鉴权
- 提供您所请求的社交、聊天、活动与商品功能
- 处理订单与提供客户支持
- 识别、预防和处置欺诈、滥用与安全问题
- 推送与服务相关的通知

### 三、信息的共享

我们不出售您的个人信息。仅在以下情况共享：
- 您主动发布的内容或发送的消息会被其他用户看到
- 运行本应用所必需的服务商（托管、推送、支付）
- 适用法律要求或为保护权利与安全所必需

### 四、信息保留

账号有效期内我们保留您的账号数据。您可随时通过下方联系方式申请删除，我们将在合理期限内删除或匿名化，法律另有要求的除外。

### 五、安全

我们采用业界标准的措施（传输加密、密码哈希、访问控制）保护您的数据。但任何传输或存储方式都不可能 100% 安全。

### 六、未成年人

NiceGo 不针对 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会有意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息，如发现将予以删除。

根据您所在地区的法律，您可能享有访问、更正、导出、删除个人数据或反对/限制某些处理的权利。请通过下方邮箱行使权利。

NiceGo 在中国境内运营。使用本应用即表示您理解您的数据可能在中国境内被处理，无论您身处何地，我们都将按本政策提供保护。

我们可能不定期更新本政策。重大变更将在应用内提示或邮件通知。
